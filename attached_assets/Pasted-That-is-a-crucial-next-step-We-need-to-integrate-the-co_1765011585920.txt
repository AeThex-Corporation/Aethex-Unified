## **Mobile App Advancement: Integration of Nexus & GameForge**

### **1. The Nexus Feature: "Gig Radar" (Monetization)**

This feature is designed to directly fuel the **Talent Flywheel** (Funnel 3) by giving creators an edge in securing contracts.

* **Goal:** Instant notification and one-tap application to job postings.
* **Location:** **Night Mode** (Creator Dashboard).
* **Feature:** **Gig Radar**
    * **Functionality:** The app utilizes push notifications to alert the user when a new job is posted on the **`NEXUS` Marketplace** that matches their **Passport Skills** (e.g., "Synthwave Composer" or "UEFN Scripter").
    * **UI:** A dedicated tab showing a simple list of matching opportunities. Swiping right on a listing triggers a "Quick Apply" (sending a pre-approved proposal template to the client).
    * **Why it works:** Good jobs vanish quickly. This gives our "Architects" a speed advantage over the general freelance market.

### **2. The GameForge Feature: "Sprint Countdown" (Execution)**

This feature enforces the **"Execute with Excellence"** principle and the **"Kill-Gate"** philosophy on the go.

* **Goal:** Provide constant, high-pressure visibility of the 30-day sprint cycle.
* **Location:** **Night Mode** (Creator Dashboard).
* **Feature:** **Active Sprint Status Widget**
    * **Functionality:** A persistent card on the main dashboard showing the countdown timer for their active `GAMEFORGE` project (e.g., "**Day 12 of 30** | Kill-Gate Passed").
    * **Integration:** The widget links directly to their embedded **Kanban board** in the mobile app, allowing for quick checks and logging of billable hours (if they are a contractor).
    * **Why it works:** Reinforces time management and urgencyâ€”shipping is the only metric that matters.

### **3. The Final Component: Shared Data Model**

Both features require the mobile app to communicate with the **`aethex.dev`** Monolith API to fetch:
* The user's **Skills/Tags** (for filtering the Gig Radar).
* The **Active Project Deadline** (for the Sprint Countdown).

This makes the mobile app a functional "Remote Control" for the entire ecosystem.
