# AeThex Companion - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Company:** AeThex  
**Tagline:** "Building the Future of Immersive Digital Experiences"  
**Target Audience:** Tech-savvy individuals, developers, businesses, and educators

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Accent Purple:** #5533FF - Primary actions, buttons, highlights
- **Accent Light:** #7755FF - Hover states, secondary highlights
- **Background Dark:** #020817 - Night mode background
- **Primary Slate:** #1E293B - Day mode accents, text

### Neutral Colors
- **Text Primary:** #F8FAFC - White text on dark backgrounds
- **Text Secondary:** #94A3B8 - Muted text, labels
- **Text Dark:** #1E293B - Text on light backgrounds
- **Border Dark:** #334155 - Borders, dividers on dark
- **Border Light:** #CBD5E1 - Borders on light backgrounds

### Semantic Colors
- **Success:** #22C55E - Completed actions, positive states
- **Warning:** #F59E0B - Caution, pending states
- **Error:** #EF4444 - Errors, destructive actions

### Background Colors
- **Day Background:** #FFFFFF
- **Day Card:** #FFFFFF
- **Day Secondary:** #F1F5F9
- **Night Background:** #020817
- **Night Card:** #0F172A
- **Night Secondary:** #1E293B

---

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary:** Courier New, Courier, monospace
- **Fallback:** System monospace fonts

### Font Sizes
- **H1:** 72px (marketing/splash only)
- **H2:** 30px (section headers)
- **Body:** 18px (standard text)
- **Small:** 14px (labels, captions)
- **Tiny:** 12px (metadata)

---

## Spacing

- **Base Unit:** 4px
- **Border Radius:** 6px (buttons), 12px (cards), 2px (inputs)
- **Card Padding:** 16px
- **Section Margins:** 20px

---

## Component Styles

### Buttons
**Primary Button:**
- Background: #5533FF
- Text: #F8FAFC
- Border Radius: 6px
- Shadow: rgba(59, 130, 246, 0.3) 0px 0px 20px

**Secondary Button:**
- Background: #020817
- Text: #F8FAFC
- Border: 1px solid #41378B
- Border Radius: 6px

### Cards
- Background: Theme-appropriate (white/dark)
- Border Radius: 12px
- Border: 1px solid theme border color
- No shadows (use background elevation)

### Inputs
- Border: 1px solid #CCCCCC
- Border Radius: 2px
- Padding: 12px

---

## Dual Mode System

### Day Mode (Oversight/Compliance)
- Light backgrounds (#FFFFFF)
- Purple accent (#5533FF)
- Professional, enterprise feel
- Used for: Admins, managers, teachers, owners

### Night Mode (Engagement/Creation)
- Dark backgrounds (#020817)
- Purple accent (#5533FF) with glow effects
- Modern, creator-focused feel
- Used for: Freelancers, contractors, students

---

## Market Contexts

### Small Business
- Day: Compliance dashboard, expense tracking, approvals
- Night: Creator bounties, guild chat, digital wallet

### K-12 Education
- Day: Admin oversight, FERPA compliance, guardian consent
- Night: Study groups, skill progression, gamification

---

## Accessibility

- Minimum touch target: 44x44px
- Color contrast: WCAG AA compliant
- Haptic feedback for actions
- VoiceOver labels for all elements
